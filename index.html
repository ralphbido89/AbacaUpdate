about us

Abaca is a senior-assisted living facility located in the city of Phoenix, Arizona. The facility is a community-based facility that provides a safe and secure environment for individuals needing daily living assistance and their caregivers. The facility is equipped with state-of-the-art technology and resources to support the well-being of the residents.
